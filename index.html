We are an arts collective based in Toronto, Canada. We believe that every place carries its own spiritual and experiential landscape — a unique emotional resonance shaped by people, history, climate, and movement. This landscape often manifests in music. When artists from the same place create, their shared environment leaves subtle traces in their sound. By listening, you might sense the atmosphere of a city without ever visiting it. We invite you to explore these questions with us: What does Toronto sound like to you?
