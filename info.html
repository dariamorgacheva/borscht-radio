About Borscht Radio

我们是一个位于加拿大多伦多的艺术团体。 We are an arts collective based in Toronto, Canada. 我们相信，每个地方都承载着独特的精神与体验景观——由人、历史、气候与流动共同塑造的情感共鸣。 We believe that every place carries its own spiritual and experiential landscape — a unique emotional resonance shaped by people, history, climate, and movement. 这种景观常常在音乐中显现。 This landscape often manifests in music. 当来自同一城市的艺术家进行创作时，他们共享的环境会在声音中留下细微的印记。 When artists from the same place create, their shared environment leaves subtle traces in their sound. 通过聆听，你或许能够在未曾造访的情况下，感受到一座城市的氛围。 By listening, you might sense the atmosphere of a city without ever visiting it. 我们邀请你与我们一起思考这些问题： We invite you to explore these questions with us: 对你来说，多伦多是什么声音？ What does Toronto sound like to you? 对你来说，新竹又是什么声音？ What does Hsinchu sound like to you? 当你聆听由这些地方孕育的音乐时，会产生怎样的感受？ What feelings emerge when you listen to music shaped by different places?
